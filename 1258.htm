Jacques Bouyssou
(1926 - 1997)

Paris La Rue
 Listed below is a beautiful oil painting by Jacques Bouyssou.  This painting depicts  a street of Paris with old buildings and people casually strolling.  The spectrum of mellow colors on the store fronts of the buildings gives pictorial charm.       The characters of the facade of the old buildings give feeling of mellowness like aging wine.  Through this painting with masterly touch of Bouyssou  the viewers sense the simple and yet happy Parisian life.  This is painting for true collector.  I remember a remark by a collector who said " Jacques Bouyssou is happy Utrillo."
