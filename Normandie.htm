..A Paris....

J'ai rencontré mon vieil ami Daron Sarhadian sur les Grands Boulevards à Paris.
C'est toujours un grand plaisir de rencontrer un ancien ami et confident.
Nous sommes allés dans un bistro et parlé de la famille, de l'art, des grands vin du Bordelais, et aussi de politique. Bref, nous avons débattu de ce qui allait mal actuellement.

 

My associate Dr. Dikran joined. He is a medical doctor researching medicine for internal organs - also specializing in colonoscopy. You will see photo of huge colonoscopy machine displayed in Pompidou Modern Art Museum.

He does not help me with my pain, but he helps me artistic expression with photography. I think he is a genius.



Simple lunch in the bistro before I start my first day in Paris - cannot be too simple with bottle of Bordeux '96.
 


We are heading for Pompidou Museum


I have been wondering many times, why the Pompidou Museum should be finished like this. 		 

I look and feel like this after a bottle of Bordeux, I am on escalator to special exhibition room. It has been my ever lasting desire to figure out what is going on with contemporary art.
