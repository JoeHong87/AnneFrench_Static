Michel-Henry

...Coquelicots...
 

 This is a beautiful flower painting by Michel-Henry. He is often compared to Fantin-Latour, the great master of floral subjects. However it is Michel-Henry's post impressionistic style with the touch of contemporary feeling that gives clarity and luminosity to his paintings. This red poppy is particularly well executed with oil on canvas. The glass vase rendered against the sea with dappling waves and small boats drawn with geographic line give cool pleasing sensation.
