André Vignoles
 (b. 1920 - )

Bouquet Clair

 

Listed below is a beautiful oil on painting by  Andre Vignoles  of his favorite subject of  bouquet of flowers.    The soft muted palette is very romantic like early morning mist. This was painted in 1968.  In late years his palette is changed to a bright tone.  This painting was acquired from an estate sale. A label from David Findlay Gallery in New York is on the back of the stretcher and marked 1968.  I reframed it with hand carved golf leafed custom frame that goes very well with the painting.
