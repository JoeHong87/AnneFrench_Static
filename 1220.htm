Jean Francois Bourgeat

French Impressionist Painter

 

…..Marais Poirevin…..

 

 Listed here is a beautiful oil painting by Jean Francois Bourgeat , a landscape of countryside in early summer time. The air is pleasantly fresh and the sun shines softly through the scattering clouds. The painting evokes a Monet like impressions. Bourgeat followed the tradition of the impressionist masters and yet developed his own style which is quite unique.

 
 

 

 

The canvas is in excellent condition and framed in hand carved custom frame.
Frame measurements: 26 1/2" x 23 1/2"
Canvas measurements: 18 x 15"

 

 


Jean Francois Bourgeat was born in Paris in 1962. He studied at the Ecole Art Graphic and the Ecole des Beaux Arts. He became attracted to the style of impressionist masters. He absorbed the techniques of the masters and he was intrigued by the scenes around Ile de France, Brittany and Alsace.
He has exhibited at the Salon Maurice in Paris and the Salon de Promodiere in Brittany and he is well represented by respectable galleries in France and America.
