Q>Can I return the painting after I purchased?
  A>Yes you can return the painting within 7 days after receipt of the painting. A refund will be processed upon receipt of the painting returned in the original   condition. The shipping and packaging costs are non-refundable and buyer pays the return shipping cost.

Q>Can I pay with credit cards?
  A>No, we do not accept credit cards. We accept payment by Paypal, personal check, bank check, wire transfer and direct deposit to our account.

Q>I want to know the painting fits on my wall and goes with the decor.
  A>You can take a photo of your wall and email to us. We will put the painting proportionately correct on your
        wall on the photo and send to you. You can see how the painting would look on your wall.  Home Viewing

Q>How do I know the painting is authentic?
  A> We sell only authentic paintings and we will issue a letter of guarantee for all the painting we sell.

Q>Can you send me the painting for approval?
  A>No we do not send the painting for approval. The presentation of the painting on our website is very accurate. The photos are professionally taken so it is almost as good as seeing the real painting.

Q>How do I know the painting is in good condition?
  A>You may ask at anytime the condition report. If there is an issue of restoration, relining, touch-up the paint loss, etc. we will disclose it to you.
 
Q>How long have you been in business?
  A>We opened the business in 2001. And ever since we have been buying and selling with integrity. We have numerous of clients with repeat business.
