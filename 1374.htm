YOLANDE ARDISSONE  

Les tulipes
 

 
Yolande Ardissone was born in Normandy in 1927, of an Italian father and a Ukrainian mother. At the age of seventeen, she left for Paris to study  at the Ecole des Arts Appliques and the Academie de la Grande Chaumiere. Two years later, she transfers to Ecole des Beaux-Arts. Soon after that, she traveled to Italy, Spain, Algeria, Holland, Indonesia, Egypt, Haiti, China, Thailand, Russia etc. She was fascinated by the kaleidoscope of different region with different climate and culture. She absorbed like a sponge the beauties and feelings and these emancipate on her canvas forcefully with matured technique influenced by Van Gogh.

 Her works have been acquired by the museums:

 Ville de Paris
 The Musée de l'Ile de France
 The Musée de la Marine
 L'Etat

 The City of Paris has awarded her:

 The Médaille d'Argent.
