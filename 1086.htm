Jacques Bouyssou
...Paris - Rue Durantin...

You are looking at a beautiful oil painting of Jacques Bouyssou depicting a street of Paris – Rue Durantin – with people strolling on the street. Many artist painted Paris streets and buildings. The building façade is well rendered with personalities. Utrillo painted many back streets of Paris and his painting shows a feeling of detachment  and loneliness while Bouyssou is very unpretentious and happy. Bouyssou was a very simple and happy person – and he conveys his inner feeling to the viewer. He depicts everyday life on the streets of Paris. It is like listening a chanson or reading a small poetry sitting at the bistro sipping aperitif.
