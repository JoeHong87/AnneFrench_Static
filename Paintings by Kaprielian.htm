Biography of Kaprielian

 

Born in France in 1959, Yetvart Kaprielian has become a Chevalier and an Associated Member of the ART section of the International Academy of Greci-Marino (Italy). Kaprielian can be classified as a new generation of impressionist
painters painting landscapes of the Ile-de-France, Normandy, Brittany and Provence, the riverbanks of Seine through all seasons.

His masterly brush strokes with luminous palette of colors captures the ever changing natures in the beautiful – serene countryside. His works have been exhibited in respectable galleries in France as well as America.  His works became part of the collection of the Royal family of Morocco.
