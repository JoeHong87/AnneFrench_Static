Raimundo Martin

 

 

 

 

 

Biography

    1936       Born, Sancti-Spiritus, Cuba

    1954-57  Studied at San Alejandro School of Fine Arts, Havana.

    1959      Studied ceramics with Amelia Pelaez, Havana.

    1960-62 Studied with Rufino Tamayo at La Esmeralda, School of Fine Arts, Mexico City

    1976      Moved to Spain to study lithography in Granada.

 

Exhibitions.

    1998      Hortt 40 Exhibition. Museum of Art, Ft. Lauderdale, Fl.

    1997      Angels and Archangels, La Boheme Fine Arts Gallery, Coral Gables, Fl.

    1991      Cuban Museum of Fine Art, Miami, Fl.

    1981      Interamericas Art Gallery, Coral Gables, Fl.

    1978      Bernal Gallery, Chicago, Ill.

    1977      Clarence White Gallery, Chicago Ill.

    1977      John Cain Gallery, Chicago, Ill.

   

    1960      El Progreso, Sancti-Spiritus, Cuba.

    1960      Circulo de Bellas Artes, Havana, Cuba.

    1960      II Bienal Interamericana, Palacio de Bellas Artes, Mexico City.

    1959      Soldevilla Art Gallery, Havana, Cuba.

    1958      Liceum, Havana, Cuba.

    1956      VIII Annual Salon Nacional, Palacio de Bellas Artes, Havana, Cuba.
