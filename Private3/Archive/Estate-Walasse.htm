Three paintings by Walasse Ting  from estate of Grayzel

This photo shows Walasse Ting in his New York Studio with his two paintings.
He wrote a letter to Alfred and Frieda offering his two paintings shown in the photo for sale for $1,200 and $1,500.
Alfred and Frieda already bought other painting from Walasse at this time. Frieda was working in Brooklyn Museum at this time and came to get acquainted Walasse.
And he mentioned it is the half the price.
I have love letters by Walasse to his girl friend Nela. And verified they are both same hand writing.
