François Gall
(1912 - 1987)
Deauville



I acquired this painting about 25 years ago from late art dealer Marumo in Paris. This is one of the finest paintings by Francois Gall done in early period. The composition is more artistic than decorative.
I specially love the tonality and Boudin like composition of dressed up people on the far side of the Deauville beach.
