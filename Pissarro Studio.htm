John Hong visited Claude Pissarro's studio outside Paris in1993
from left Claude, Lyonel (son of Claude) and John.
Claude was developing a new look - apart from his grand-father Camille Pissarro, the large canvas on the easel on left was the contemporary look he was working on, and the small canvas on the right hand side is the traditional style. I acquired this painting - showing on this website.

 



John was reading and translating a letter from Han Woo Lee - a prominent artist in Korea. Eventually John organized a joint two men show in Seoul Korea.
The show opened in 1995 and the event was sponsored by Korea Daily News as a part of cultural exchange between France and Korea. From left to right Claude, Lyonel, Katia and John.

Announcement card of new born baby.

Claude was very happy and excited. He painted this painting with large and small sneakers, on the
large sneaker, there is a name of the new born baby Isaak.
