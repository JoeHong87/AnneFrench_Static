Jacques Bouyssou
(1926 - 1997)

FRENCH OFFICIAL MARINE PAINTER

...Amsterdam - Canal Rokim...
 

You are looking at a beautiful oil painting of Jacques Bouyssou depicting canal with bridge in Amsterdam.  The façades of the buildings along the canal in different colors and  the draw-bridge make a wonderful composition. This is the reminiscence of the draw-bridge painting by Vincent Van Gogh who painted in this area. There are movements on the water, seagulls flying - this is very classic Bouyssou painting.
