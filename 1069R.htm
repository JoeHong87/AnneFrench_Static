SUZANNE EISENDIECK
 (1926 - 1997)

..Ballerines avant L'entreee en scene....
Listed below is the beautiful oil painting by Suzanne Eisendieck depicting ballerinas posing before the scene starts. The  composition and the bright sensational palette are just wonderful that there is no need further elaboration of this master piece. This is the best of her works I have ever seen. The masterly brush stroke is the reminiscence of Renoir.


..Ballerines avant L'entreee en scene....

Oil on canvas -  23 1/2"  x  36"
  Hand carved French style frame measures  32 1/2" x 45"
