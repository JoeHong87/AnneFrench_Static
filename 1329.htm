ARDISSONE, YOLANDE - (b. 1927)

...Sail Boats ...

Listed below is the beautiful oil painting by Yolande Ardissone depicting peaceful  bay with sail boats. Ardissone made a wonderful composition with  water, sky, sail boats and the houses on the hill.  She captured the pleasant atmosphere and displayed it on her canvas with masterly touch.
