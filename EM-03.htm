Giner Bueno
(b. 1935 - )
Children playing in the water


Oil on canvas  -    18 1/8" x 25 5/8"   -   frame  - 29" x 36 1/2"
  Sold

Giner Bueno was born in 1935 in a small coastal town near Valencia and he studied at La Academia de Bellas Artes of San Carlos and later went to Paris to further develop artistic talent.He was influenced by the tutelage of his father, Luis Giner Vallas, a protege of late Spanish master Joaquin Sorrolla.
Bueno`s passionate brushwork reveals his passion for the land and the people of his native Valencia. Many of Giner Bueno`s oil paintings have images of fishermen plying their trade in handmade boats and women dressed in traditional apparel gathering the day`s catch. Each painting is a study of color, form, and light within a landscape dominated by an ever-changing Mediterranean climate.His paintings were exhibited in numerous museums including Museum of Valencia, the Museo Real Maestranza de Cablleria de Seville, etc
