Raimundo Martin is a Surrealist Cuban artist. He was born in Sancti Spiritus Cuba in 1936. He entered San Alejandro School of Fine Arts in Havana 1954 and studied for four years. In 1960 he went to Mexico City and studied painting under modern master Rufino Tamayo at  La Esmeralda School of Fine Arts.

He developed his artistic vision and philosophy in Cuba during the 1950's and 60's,where he associated with other artist in Havana. He has evolved to another dimension of intellectual and sensual Alchemy, independently creating his own niche of modern surrealism.

I am very happy to feature for our collectors 4 paintings recently completed by Raimundo Martin.  Each painting is rendered with beautiful touch of nature - the trees, palm trees, moon,  sky and water composed in surrealism and then fusion with abstract borders.  The artist put his boundless surrealistic imagination in symmetry and geometrical forms.
