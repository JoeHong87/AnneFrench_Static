Jacques Bouyssou
 (1926 - 1997)
Entre du port de Trouville (1972)

Oil on Canvas (#1260) -  28 3/4" x 36 1/4"   -  frame  36 1/2"  x 44"

If you have any question,        Please Ask >



Listed here is a beautiful oil painting by Jacques Bouyssou.  This painting depicts  the passage to the  port of Trouville with fishing boats sailing pleasantly.  There are people on the jetty with the light house.  This is really beautiful seascape.   Jacques Bouyssou   loved to paint seascapes visiting different  ports of France and other parts Europe, and eventually he earned the title of Official Marine Painter of France and participated annual exhibition at the  marine museum in Paris - Musee de la Marine.
