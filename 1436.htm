Jacques Bouyssou  (1926 - 1997)
 Moret sur Loing

Bouyssou captured the crisp atmosphere of  beginning pf autumn  in Moret sur Loing - outside Paris. The beauty of this region inspired many artists - specially  Monet, Renoir and Sisley.  You can view Alfred Sisley's painting of Moret sur Loing on the Web Museum. In this painting - the brush stroke of Bouyssou is spontaneous, free and sincere.  He just instilled the beauty of the surrounding area in this canvas. The composition is wonderful with Seine river with bridge and train just crossed. The house with red roof and mother and child are strolling on the river bank. This is a very large painting in an impressive museum quality frame.       scroll down
