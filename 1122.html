Charles Levier
(1920 - 2003)
 

Devant un Cinema
 

 

 

The canvas size is 40 x 30 inches.
The frame size is 46  x  36 inches

Listed here is the original oil painting by Charles Levier, depicting three girls standing in front of Cinema. The blank expression of the girls dressed in fur trimmed coat dress is interesting. The geometric lines in the foreground, square window panes of the cinema with glowing amber interior lights create sensation a la Levier.  All elements in the composition give Levier like mystique. This is classic Levier painting that we love.

This beautiful painting is appropriately framed in hand carved custom frame finished in antique silver tone and the frame is in mint condition.
