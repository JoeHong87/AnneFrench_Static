ARDISSONE, YOLANDE - (b. 1927)

...Flower blooming Village above the Bay...

Listed below is the beautiful oil painting by Yolande Ardissone depicting small flower blooming village with houses above peaceful bay.  Ardissone made a wonderful composition with  water, sail boats, houses and flowering trees on the hill.  She captured the pleasant atmosphere and displayed it on her canvas with masterly touch. Her brush stroke is spontaneous and palette is pleasant.
