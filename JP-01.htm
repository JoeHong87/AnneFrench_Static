Jacques Bouyssou
(1926 - 1997)

Paris le Pont Royal - circa late 1980s

 

Jacques Bouyssou captured the beautiful scene of bridge of Paris  - Pont Royal over Seine river.  Towards late 1980s Bouyssou's palette turned much brighter and happier. His series of Paris streets, bridges and monuments are truly magnificent and sought  after by collectors all around the world.  This is Jacques Bouyssou at his best -  crème de la crème.
