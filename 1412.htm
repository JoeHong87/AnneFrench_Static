Michel-Henry
 ...Arashiyama...

 Arashiyama is an area outside Kyoto and also refers to the mountain across the Oi River.   You see in this painting the Togetsukyo Bridege - the famous landmark in this area. Simple flower arrangement in a Japanese vase with the background of Arashiyama mountain and a bridge crossing the river is really serene setting for this painting. Michel Henry once again displayed his artistic sensibility.
