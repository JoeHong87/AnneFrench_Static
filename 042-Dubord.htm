Jean Pierre Dubord

Harbour Brest

The weather - the sky, wind, temperature and the atmosphere is constantly changing in Normandie - gives different moods.
The artist has to capture the mood instantly and put on the canvas - the master work that lasts forever.
Here the impressionism was born - Jean Pierre Dubord bears the torch to carry on the impressionism to next generation.

Oil on canvas   - 23 5/8"  x  28 3/4"   (60cm x 73cm)  20F
Exhibited in Salon de la Marine 1992 / from the personal collection of artist
