Robert Philipp
(1895 -1981)
 An American in Paris

               Oil on canvas   -   30" x  35"      -  frame   -     40"  x   45"

This is last finished painting by Robert Philipp before his death in 1981 at the age
of 86. The painting was kept in personal collection of Ross Peacock. Peacock was
a life-time dealer for Philipp.
