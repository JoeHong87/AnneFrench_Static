Robert Philipp
(1895 -1981)

Tea time in Gloucester
 




Oil on Canvas (#1008)  -   30 1/2  x  46 1/2 inches
frame size  40 1/2  x  57 inches

 

 
 

This rare quality  oil painting is  from a personal collection of prominent retired New York art dealer Ross Peacock who represented Philipp for many years.    

Philipp as a teacher at the Art Students League for over thirty years and at the National Academy for sixteen years, was an important influence on American art. He was elected as a full member of National Academy in 1945 and his works are widely represented by numerous museums including Metropolitan, Whitney, Cocoran, etc.

 

 He was a member of :

 The Lotus Club
 National Academy  of  Design
 Royal Society of Arts

 

 His works are in the collections of :

 The Cocoran Gallery, Washington
 Columbus Museum of  Fine Arts, Ohio
 High Museum, Atlanta Georgia
 Dallas Museum Texas
 Metropolitan Museum of Art, New York
 Whitney Museum of Art, New York
