Jean Pierre Dubord
(b. 1949 - )

Rouen - Soleil sur la Seine


 Here is a beautiful oil on painting by Jean Pierre Dubord  of his favorite subject of  Rouen with Seine River with barges. The sun is shining through the hazy atmosphere and reflects on the river.  Dubord's impressionistic touch is very poetic.  This is a small painting but shows the masterly touch of  the artist.     This is lovely composition.

 On Jacques Bouyssou -

Jacques Bouyssou  provides a personal vision of Eternal Paris where, for centuries, Notre-Dame has been on guard, in the heart of the city. - S. Lemaire

Bouyssou was right when he said : “ I paint what I see and what I am.” Painting shouldn’t be an assault on nature, overloading it with who knows what kind of construction of the mind. It should be an appeal, such as that made by past craftsmen, searching to uncover the secrets of wood, clay and water. It means trying to reach the heart of mystery…            - - Felix Vercel

Drawing is the integrity of an artist. There are no touch ups and overlays. The chalk line flows like a verdict. And it is truth to the heart. I was deeply moved when Bouyssou made the drawing for me.  -  John Hong
