Jean Pierre Dubord
 (b. 1949 - )

Sur la plage en ete
 Here is a beautiful oil on painting by Jean Pierre Dubord  of his favorite subject of  Normande beach in summer time.   The peoples are sitting down leisurely on the beach and there are sail boats and  people swimming in the sea. The sky is beautiful against the old beach houses.   This is lovely composition.  He composed the scene with masterly impressionistic touch. It just is a beautiful poetry of a summer time. We see again the genius of Dubord in this beautiful painting.
