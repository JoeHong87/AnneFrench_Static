RAIMUNDO MARTIN

Espanol  


 

 

Born in Sancti Spiritus, Cuba in 1936. Studied drawing in San Alejandro art school
in Havana and at  La Esmeralda art school in Mexico City, where he investigated
the fresco technique and color theory. Throughout his life he has worked in places
such as Havana, Mexico City, Granada (Spain), Chicago, Barcelona, and finally
Miami, where he lives and creates.
