Jean Pierre Dubord

Le soir sur la Cote Normande

 Here is a beautiful oil on painting by Jean Pierre Dubord  of his favorite subject of   Normandy Beach in summer time.  This time he depicts the beach at the end of the day when the evening starts to set in.  The atmosphere is calm and some boats are still on the sea and returning and there is tremendous tranquility. The sun is set but the sea is still luminous and Dubord captured the tranquility at end of the summer day with masterly touch.     It really is a very much poetic composition which is a trade mark of Dubord.
