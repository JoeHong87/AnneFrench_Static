CONSIGNMENT

Anne French accepts quality paintings on consignment for sale.
Here is how it works:

The consignment platform is where seller and buyer negotiate and make deal directly. Anne French encourages the seller to  ask reasonable fair market price by providing current market information to the seller. Anne French also encourage the buyer to make a reasonable offer to the seller and start negotiating. Buyer may ask Anne French an expert opinion about the painting. Anne French will provide unbiased opinion. When seller and buyer reached an agreement to finalize the deal, Anne French will send the invoice according to the agreed terms and conditions to the buyer. Buyer makes the payment to Anne French and seller ship the painting to the buyer. When buyer receives the painting and finds the painting is in the same condition as described by the seller, then Anne French send the payment to the seller. Anne French charges only to seller 12% of the sales price.

FAQ

Q. Why I use Anne French platform instead of sending the painting to auction house?
    A.  Auction house usually charges 15% to the seller (seller fee) and 25% the buyer (buyer's premium), 1.5% insurance, catalog (photograph) fee $150 -$500.   You have to ship the painting to auction house - crating and shipping cost $200 - $1000 depends on the size and value of your painting.
         In case the painting does not sell (does not meet the minimum reserve), it has to come back to you incurring shipping charges. Using Anne French platform, no expenses will incur except 12% fee to the seller when the sales are realized.  

Q. Why I use Anne French platform instead of selling on Ebay?
    A. Anybody can have any painting on Ebay for sale. Anne French dos not accept all the painting on consignment. Anne French set high standard of criteria in accepting consignment so the buyer has a level of confidence in purchasing on Anne French platform. Anne French also provides expert opinion - totally  unbiased. The difference is like comparing bazaar and specialty store.

Q.  Why I use Anne French platform instead of consigning to main street gallery?
    A.  Main street gallery is localized. They sell to client who walk in the gallery and they may sell to their established clients in the area. Anne French is global. We are known for our specialty on 20th century French art and visitors come to our site from all over the world.

Q.  How long does it take until the painting is sold?
    A. It all depends on the artist / subject matter / quality of the painting / how much you are asking for.  Usually a good painting by a popular artist in reasonable price sells within 1 - 3 months.

Q.  Can I trust Anne French? Will Anne French send me the money when painting is sold?
    A.  Anne French is established in 2001 as a fine art dealer - with specialty in 20th century French art - and enjoyed a good reputation and followings of repeat clients. You may see client's feed back.
