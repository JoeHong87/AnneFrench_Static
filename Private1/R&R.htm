Dear Rita & Richard

It was a delightful experience to have a chance to see some of your wonderful collection. As a collector myself, I was thrilled and overwhelmed to see the stunning collection in depth. I could sense the passion and love of art in you and the delightful sensation you had when you found and acquired each one of the items.
But like anything else in life, there is a time to depart. I used the analogy of the feeling you have when your daughter is getting married.
When the feeling is such, I just cannot lightly make suggestions to promote or dispose of your collection. I will give some serious thought over the next few days, and make some proposal by next week. Regardless whatever way we choose, I will definitely purchase more than several pieces to add to my collection.

It was a lovely lunch at the Club, and I really enjoyed the food, the company and the view.

I quickly put up a page to show you an example of how your collection should be presented in the website, just in case I would represent you to make deals with various clients.

I will be in touch in the beginning of the week.

Kindest regards,

Charles Levier
(1920 - 2003)
 

 

Listed here is the original oil painting by Charles Levier, depicting three girl sitting on a table in front of the window.  She is in solitude and one curious bird peeps inside her room. A loaf of bread and half empty bottle of wine and bouquet of flowers put aside on the table make a very interesting composition. The pictorial mood is further deepened with her mysterious expression on her face. Here we can see the masterly touch of Levier at his best.   This is just a wonderful manifestation of Levier genius on canvas.  

This beautiful painting is appropriately framed in hand carved custom frame finished in antique gold tone and the frame is in good  condition.
