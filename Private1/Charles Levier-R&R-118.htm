Charles Levier
(1920 - 2003)

(a lonely bird with the flowers)
 

Listed here is the original oil painting by Charles Levier, depicting flowers in the vase with a visiting lonely bird watching. The castle is on the hill and there is a ocean in the far distance. Levier changed his style several times in his career. This painting is believed to be done in the late 80s. The composition is delicately calm and the colors are beautiful - dream like. It seems that finally peace and tranquility settled in Levier's soul. And yet the lone bird adds a little whimsy.

 

 



canvas size -  20" x 24"
