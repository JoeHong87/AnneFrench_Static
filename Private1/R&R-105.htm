Charles Levier
(1920 - 2003)
....A la Campagne..

On a beautiful weekend Levier girl is visiting countryside with bouquet of flowers -
wearing a beautiful dress and flower earring.
Her face is lightly blushed like a rose petal.        
A few girls in the foreground leisurely taking a walk on the country lane.
The lane leading far away to the eternity and the aspiration of Levier girl.
