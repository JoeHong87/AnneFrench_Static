Charles Levier
(1920 - 2003)
 

Listed here is the original oil painting by Charles Levier, depicting a girl sitting at the table in front of the window.  She is in solitude and one curious bird peeps inside her room. A loaf of bread and half empty bottle of wine and bouquet of flowers put aside on the table make a very interesting composition. The pictorial mood is further deepened with her mysterious expression on her face. Here we can see the masterly touch of Levier at his best.   This is just a wonderful manifestation of Levier genius on canvas.
