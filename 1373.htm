YOLANDE ARDISSONE  

Fleurs et Bateaux

 

This is a lovely painting - one of the best painted by Yolande Ardissone. The palette is bright and spontaneous and there are all the elements of impressionism at its best. The scene is in Normandy - a bay with boats seen through wild flowers. The viewer can feel the typical Normandy spring weather with the scattered clouds high up in the sky drifting swiftly.
