Jean Pierre Dubord
 (b. 1949 - )

Les enfants devant les Maneges


 Here is a beautiful oil on painting by Jean Pierre Dubord  of his favorite subject of  country fair with carousel.   The flags are flying,  many people with children are around and the atmosphere is quite cheerful.  This is lovely composition.  He composed the scene with masterly impressionistic touch. It just is a beautiful poetry of a summer time. We see again the genius of Dubord in this beautiful painting.
