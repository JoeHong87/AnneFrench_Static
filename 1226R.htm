Jean Pierre Dubord
 (b. 1949 - )

Rouen - Rue Francis Yard


Listed below is a beautiful oil on painting by  Jean Pierre Dubord  of his favorite subject of  suburb of Rouen.    The famous cathedral  is in the background.  He depicts the street with the old houses and a few people strolling.  Dubord's masterly touch with his unique style evokes a poetic feeling. This  painting is gem, a good example of his best works.
