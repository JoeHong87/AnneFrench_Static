Buy / Sell

  

We welcome all inquiries regarding the purchase or sale of paintings. Please feel free to either call us at (305) 632-2032 or send us an email at info@anne-french.com .

To purchase a painting from Anne French :

In order to provide the highest quality collecting experience, we strive to describe our paintings as accurately and in as much detail as possible.
The photos you find on our site are of professional quality and are as vivid and as clear as can be presented over the Internet. 
Many of our client collectors purchase their paintings from the detailed images we provide. Once delivered, the majority of the time, these paintings surpass our clients' expectations.

If, however,  you would like to see our collection or a particular painting in person, you are encouraged to come visit us. Please call to set up a private viewing appointment.

Payment :
We accept personal checks, casher's checks, and wire transfers. We also accept payments via Paypal.

Packaging and Shipping :
We carefully pack your painting in a custom-built wooden box that provides the highest level of safety for your piece. We ship the package via Federal Express. The shipping and packaging cost will be charged to the buyer. This cost will vary depending on the size of the painting and where it is shipped. A typical charge might be in the range of $100 - $300 for shipping within the continental United States.   

International shipment :

We have no control over customs duties and VATs at destination countries. The buyer is responsible for paying these charges. Generally, Federal Express will clear the customs on your behalf and they will advise you of and collect from you, the duty and VAT.

Guarantee:

All of our artwork is carefully screened, examined, and vetted by art experts. We guarantee the authenticity of the paintings in our collection. A Letter of Guarantee will accompany the shipment of every painting.

Return Policy :

Buyer must contact us within 7 days of receiving their painting to request a return.  Late return requests will not be accepted. A refund will be processed upon receipt of the painting returned in the original condition.  The shipping and packaging costs are non-refundable and the buyer pays the return shipping cost.
For international shipment, returns will not be accepted.


To sell your painting :

If you are interested in selling a piece of art in your possession, please contact us.
We buy artwork outright and also work with clients to sell their pieces on consignment.
Please send us a photograph of your painting (preferably in digital format) and the following information:

- The canvas size of the painting without the frame.
- The condition of the painting surface and canvas.
- When and where the painting was acquired.
- Any additional information you feel may help in selling the painting.
